Trippie Redd - Trip At Knight

'Trip At Knight' is artist Trippie Redd's latest album, for this album cover I had a very clear image of how I wanted it to come out. I wanted to change the style of it from the official cover as the official cover uses this surreal aesthetic, the same style that he uses for most of his other covers as well which is why I wanted to create something new. I really liked the way how the typography of the text came out and overall I find that the cover also fits the name of the album quite well.
